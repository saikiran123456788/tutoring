Who we serve ?

In Southeast Los Angeles, SCA supports a broad spectrum of underserved individuals, including low-income families, immigrants, marginalized students, English Language Learners, at-risk youth, and adult learners. These groups face various challenges, such as financial constraints, language and cultural barriers, systemic discrimination, and a lack of access to educational resources. SCA's programs aim to address these issues, offering tailored support to help community members overcome obstacles and achieve success in their educational and personal development endeavors.

Why High Impact Tutoring?

High-Impact Tutoring is proven to accelerate student learning, surpassing other interventions. It promotes equity by providing access to quality tutoring for low-income and minority students, narrowing achievement gaps. With individualized instruction, it fosters self-efficacy and a love for learning. It significantly boosts graduation rates by targeting key academic milestones. Moreover, it serves as a career development opportunity for tutors, potentially leading them into teaching professions.

What is High Impact Tutoring?

High-impact tutoring is a dynamic, personalized approach that transforms the educational experience by tailoring learning to each student's unique needs, strengths, and challenges. This method involves frequent, intensive interactions between tutors and students, leveraging data-informed instruction to pinpoint and address areas for improvement. It emphasizes building strong, supportive relationships that foster a positive learning environment, alongside focusing on developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills. High-impact tutoring also incorporates social-emotional support, setting and monitoring progress toward achievable goals, and utilizing innovative technologies to enrich the learning process. By offering a holistic learning experience that extends beyond traditional subject tutoring, it integrates real-world applications and interdisciplinary connections.

What is High Impact Tutoring?

High-impact tutoring has been shown to be 20 times more effective than standard tutoring models for math and 15 times more effective for reading. Studies demonstrate that the practice increases students’ learning by an additional three to 15 months across grade levels. The University of Chicago Urban Labs tested Saga’s impact via large-scale randomized controlled trials. They found that:

  • Students learn up to 2.5 years of math in one academic year, and math course failures are reduced by up to 63%.
  • Course failures in non-tutored subjects are reduced by up to 26%.
  • Student attendance increased by as much as 18 days per year.

These unparalleled results show that high-impact tutoring is a clear solution for improving educational equity and helping students succeed.

Results of High Impact Tutoring

High-impact tutoring has proven to be a powerful tool in enhancing students' academic performance and socio-emotional development across various backgrounds, according to studies from prestigious institutions like Harvard, Brown, and Johns Hopkins Universities. These studies highlight not only the academic benefits, such as significant gains in standardized test scores and the narrowing of achievement gaps between socio-economic groups, but also the positive effects on students' self-confidence, motivation, and overall well-being. Furthermore, high-impact tutoring has been linked to higher graduation rates and the potential for increased lifetime earnings, indicating that the benefits far outweigh the initial costs. In essence, this body of research showcases the substantial, multifaceted returns on investment in high-impact tutoring programs, making a compelling case for their broader implementation.
